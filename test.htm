    Collar Button Defect

    Buyer beware. The collar buttons are placed too low on the shirt and therefore do not correctly line up with the button holes on the collar. I was informed by Everlane that this was a purposefully made "style/fit choice". Which makes about as much sense as having a pocket on the back of a jacket. Due to the collar buttons and button holes not lining up correctly it causes the fabric to bunch up in odd ways and causes more strain on the fabric around the collar buttons.
    And as reference, my slim fit style Ralph Lauren Oxfords have collar buttons and button holes that line of up correctly.

    Their idea of making good on this bizarre design error was to offer that I could return the shirts and spend more money on different shirts. As it stands, I will not spend another dime at Everlane.
